Premium Wireless Headphones

$99.99

Experience crystal-clear sound with our premium wireless headphones. Featuring noise cancellation, 30-hour battery life, and comfortable over-ear design.

Inline Embed Code:

<div id="cod-checkout-app"></div> <script src="https://your-app-url.vercel.app/embed.js" data-shop="your-store.myshopify.com" data-product-title="{{ product.title }}" data-product-image="{{ product.featured_image | img_url: 'medium' }}" data-product-price="{{ product.price | money_without_currency }}" data-currency="{{ shop.currency }}" data-mode="inline"> </script>
